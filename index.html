Method

How It Works

MediaPipe Pose Landmarker (Full) detects 33 body landmarks. Whole-body centre of mass (COM) is estimated using segment mass fractions from de Leva (1996).

Record → Process: Unlike real-time approaches, this app records video at the phone's full native frame rate (30–60 fps) with zero dropped frames, then processes every frame offline using the more accurate full model. This gives better landmark accuracy and higher effective sampling rate than real-time analysis.

Calibration: Subject stands still; known height establishes pixel-to-metre scale. A correction factor (÷ 0.935) is applied because MediaPipe's highest landmark is the nose, which sits at ~93.5% of true standing height.

Jump height = peak COM − standing COM.

Key Frames

Countermovement: Lowest COM before take-off.
Take-off: COM begins upward flight.
Peak: Maximum COM displacement.
Landing: COM returns to near standing height.

Tips

• Phone stationary (tripod)
• Film from the front (frontal plane)
• Full body visible at all times
• Good lighting
• Fitted clothing
• ~2–4 m distance
